start - starts the bot
spaces - shows list of spaces
ping - checks connectivity
count - simple counter
do - adds items to a TO-DO list
buy - adds items to a TO-BUY list
watch - adds items to a TO-WATCH list
calendar - shows calendar
today - shows today's events
tomorrow - shows tomorrow's events
yesterday - shows yesterday's events
contacts - manage your contacts
members - manage members of the current space
debts - manages your debts
lists - To-Do, To-Buy & To-Watch lists
settings - manage app settings
